/// Mission

Goals &
Aims

The Ambassador Program exists to grow the Logic Packs community through education, advocacy, and real impact.

Grow the Community

Introduce developers, students, and creators to the Logic Packs ecosystem. Host meetups, workshops, and online sessions in your region.

Educate & Empower

Teach others how to build with packs — from first prototype to production apps. Create tutorials, guides, and code-alongs.

Advocate & Feedback

Be the voice of your community. Share feedback with the LP team, shape the roadmap, and represent developers in your region.

Build & Ship

Lead by example — build real apps on Logic Packs, publish packs to the marketplace, and showcase what's possible.

Content & Reach

Create content — blogs, videos, tweets, threads — that spreads awareness and demonstrates the power of the pack ecosystem.

Bridge & Connect

Connect local developers with the global LP ecosystem — universities, hackathons, tech communities, and startup circles.

/// Tiers

Ambassador Levels

Level up as you contribute more. Each tier unlocks additional perks and responsibilities.

Bronze Ambassador

Entry level · 0–3 months

Welcome kit, digital certificate, private Slack access, $100/mo credits, 1 event budget.

Silver Ambassador

Active · 3–6 months

Premium swag drops, featured on website, $200/mo credits, 3 event budgets, speaker invitations.

Gold Ambassador

Leadership · 6+ months

All Silver perks + annual summit invite, LP team 1:1s, co-branded content, reference letters, $500/mo credits, Ventures priority access.

Platinum Ambassador

Elite · By invitation

All Gold perks + revenue share on referred signups, equity pathway, advisory role, travel sponsorship, LPDN hardware kit.
